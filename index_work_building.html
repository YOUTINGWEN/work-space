蓋幾面牆 Building some Walls

錄像投影｜實驗聲響 Video Projection | Experimental Sound

2022

因為什麼，讓我可以待在這裡。
物被光瞄準，蓋起眼睛外的辨認，安心了就待著。
我總想著，邀請那些西方人物、小動物到牆內待著，吞進皮膚內。
掠過耳邊的建構聲，竄逃似的。讓我發現我蓋了圍不起的牆。

Why can I stay here. The object is aimed by the light, covering my identification. Oh, here I can stay. I want to invite those who I can’t catch to come and stay in the wall for a while. Trying to make my wall take everything. They skim over my ears only made me realize that I had built unenclosed walls.
