耕作一塊可以賣的地 Cultivating a piece of Land for Sale

影音裝置｜三頻道播放｜彩色｜三聲道 three-channel video | three-channel audio | sculpture

防蟲塵網、遮陽網、防草塑膠布、竹竿、帆布 insect dust net, sunshade net, weed control plastic sheet, bamboo poles, canvas

2023

身處地方，重置場景中的各式關係。

風與農作遮陽網、防草塑膠布發生摩擦，
身體與竹竿在地面上尋找平衡，
施工聲響與風景彼此應和，
物質與售字立牌相互交涉。

過程中，土地的樣子越發模糊，於是我繼續向它提問。

"Cultivating a piece of Land for Sale" discusses the relationship between humans and the land.
The landscape evolves with the progress of life. The prominently visible "For Sale" sign standing on the land in Sanxing Township reveals to me the negotiated relationship constructed by humans through a shared language. Through my bodily actions, I intervene in the local environment, resetting various relationships between materials. Process the wind interacts with sunshade nets or weed control plastic sheets, create collisions between my body, bamboo, and stones, and reunite words with sound. Coming together, the process of erecting the sign is a contradictory act that cannot establish a transactional relationship.
In this action, as if all events unfold in an unexpected manner, defying expectations.

《耕作一塊可以賣的地》由宜蘭縣政府文化局典藏，所有權及著作財產權為宜蘭縣政府文化局所有。
