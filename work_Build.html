建造一間可以賣的房子 Building a house can be sold

雙頻道錄像｜單聲道｜立體雕塑 two-channel video | sculpture

廢棄建材、混凝土、鋼筋、紙漿、木板

2024

「我正在施工，為了建造一間房子，也為了販售一間房子。」

當建物被標示出售,價格、坪數、地段與機能緊隨其後，從販售行為覺察人類以特定觀點操控物件價值。本創作正在販售一間房子，同時,讓勞動中的身體與建材相互建構:揉洗、刮磨、塑造，互動過程物質性鬆動了「售」字傳遞的絕對關係,無法理直氣壯地宣告對房子的特定詮釋，單一價值失語、模糊。對生活寓居場所的多向詮釋，它們之間——相異、共構、矛盾，彼此辯解。

一場遊走在販售行為、寓居城市、房子建材的交換遊戲。

"I am under construction, both to build a house and to sell a house."

When a building is marked for sale, its price, size, location,and functionality follow suit, reflecting how humans manipulate the value of objects from specific perspectives through the act of selling. This creation is in the process of selling a house while simultaneously allowing the laboring body and construction materials to mutually shape each other—kneading, scraping, molding. Through these interactions, the materiality destabilizes the absolute relationship conveyed by the word “sale.” It becomes impossible to assert a definitive interpretation of the house, as singular values become muted and blurred. The multifaceted interpretations of a living space—differing, co-constructing, and conflicting—engage in a dialogue with one another.

An exchange game that navigates between the acts of selling, inhabiting cities, and building materials for houses.
